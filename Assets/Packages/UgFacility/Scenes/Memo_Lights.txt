----------------------------------------------------------------
Before opening the demo scene, increase Pixel light Count to 10-20.

Edit > ProjectSettings > Quality > Pixel light Count

[HDRP]

Before opening the demo scene, increase Maximum Shadows to 300.

 HDRP > Lighting > Shadows > Maximum Shadows on Screen


----------------------------------------------------------------
If the demo scene viewport is running slow, set occlusion culling.

-Bake OcclusionCulling
--Windows>Rendering>OcclusionCulling

-Camera setting
--Scene View Camera > OcclusionCulling  (the camera icon in the upper right corner of the viewport)
